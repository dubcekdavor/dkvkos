About DKVKOS

DKVKOS offers bright, freshly renovated apartments in the town of Pag. Everything you need for a relaxing seaside stay is within walking distance—beach, bakeries, cafés, and the stone streets of the Old Town.

We host couples, families and small groups year-round and speak English & Croatian.
